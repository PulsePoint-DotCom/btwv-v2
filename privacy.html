Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 20, 2026

Before They Went Viral operates the website before-they-went-viral.pages.dev. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our website and the choices you have associated with that data.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you voluntarily submit it through the contact form. The only information we collect is:

  • Name, email address, and message content submitted via the contact form
  • Basic analytics data such as pages visited, time spent on pages, and general location (country/region level only) — collected automatically by our hosting provider

How We Use Your Information

Any information you voluntarily provide through our contact form is used solely to respond to your message. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties.

Cookies

We use basic cookies to understand how visitors use our site. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings — doing so will not break the site but some features may not function as smoothly.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare Pages — our hosting provider, which may collect basic server logs
  • Formspree — our contact form processor. When you submit the contact form, your message data is sent to Formspree. Their privacy policy applies: formspree.io/legal/privacy-policy
  • Google Fonts — font loading which may log requests. Their privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

Analytics

We rely on our hosting provider's basic analytics and do not use standalone analytics platforms (such as Google Analytics) at this time. Any analytics data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

Links to Other Sites

Our website contains links to external signals and sources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies when you visit them.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.
